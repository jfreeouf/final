SPACE

Our light-filled, multi-level 10,000 sq ft space in Bushwick, Brooklyn hosts ongoing exhibitions and is available for rent as an event space. Rental inquiries: please contact rent@arthouse.com.

About us

Arthouse was formed by a group of young artists as a special project to make affordable handmade art. All the works for sale here are created specifically for this project, are hand-drawn, hand-painted or hand-crafted, and are priced by the artist who created the piece as affordably as possible. We use this venue as a creative outlet for ourselves, to pursue personal expression, to experiment in different techniques or subject matter, and to create profit in order to support ourselves. Our goal is to encourage appreciation and ownership of art, especially among those who have felt priced out of or intimidated by the art world.
